Bio

Bladee (Benjamin Reichwald) is a Swedish rapper and singer born in 1994. A founding member of the collective Drain Gang alongside Ecco2k, Whitearmor, and Thaiboy Digital, he is one of the pioneers of the underground internet rap scene. Known for his ethereal, melancholic sound and abstract lyrics, Bladee has released numerous projects since 2014 and built a devoted global fanbase.
