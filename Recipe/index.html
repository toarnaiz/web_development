How To Make a PB & J Sandwich

I will be teaching you how to make the highest quality PB & J sandwich

Ingredients

  1. Peanut Butter
  2. Bread
  3. Jelly
  4. Knife
  5. Toaster

Instructions

  1. Use knife to cut bread into two thin pieces
  2. Gently place pieces of bread into toaster
  3. Toast for 240 seconds
  4. Use Knife to place 3 ounce of Jelly on one piece of toast
  5. Use Knife to place 3 ounce of peanut butter on one piece of toast
  6. Place the Jelly toast on top of the peanut butter toast
  7. Cut a diagonal line through sandwich
  8. Enjoy!
